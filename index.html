About Me

My name is Charles Goodwin. I am a lawyer and I am a programmer. And I am the founding principal of Armigeri LLC. Armgeri - Latin for esquire - is an information technologies and services firm focusing on the legal market and allied fields. Armigeri seeks to deliver the highest quality software and web products that allow users to focus on their work, rather than wrestle with technology. We provide both commercial and custom software. Our first-to-market product is Designate, software for designating transcribed testimony. Designate automates a wretched task that lawyers hate. (See “Designations” Sidebar)

Designations

For everyone who is not a lawyer:

Lawyers select – or, as lawyers say, “designate” – testimony to draw the court's attention to the passages in the testimony that supports their client's position (or at least so the lawyers urge). When testimony is transcribed into print, every line on a page is numbered. And when testimony is submitted to the judge, the lawyers have to direct the judge to each line and page.

Lawyers thus spend hours highlighting printed or electronic transcripts. The lawyer - or more likely, his staff - takes the marked transcript and prepares a list of the page and line numbers.
